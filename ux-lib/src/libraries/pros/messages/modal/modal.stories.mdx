## Comment déclencher une fenêtre modal

Vous pouvez utiliser l'attribut `target-id` sur un composant `ux-btn` en lui attribuant la valeur de l'id de la modal
à déclencher.

<Preview withSource="none" withToolbar>
  <Story name="How to trigger modal" height="680px">
    {stories.howToTriggerModal()}
  </Story>
</Preview>

```html
<ux-btn target-id="wawa">Trigger modal</ux-btn>

<ux-modal id="wawa">
  <h1 slot="modal-title">wawa modal title</h1>
  <ux-text>
    Lorem ipsum dolor sit amet consectetur adipisicing elit. Qui perspiciatis
    iusto, accusantium esse molestias ipsum autem atque et quod dolorem quidem
    assumenda distinctio aliquam deserunt officiis perferendis asperiores eius

    laboriosam quisquam nostrum, recusandae at sunt eum dignissimos quasi

    assumenda adipisci. Aliquid, quas!
  </ux-text>
  <ux-btn-group>
    <ux-btn model="secondary">secondary</ux-btn>
    <ux-btn model="primary">primary</ux-btn>
  </ux-btn-group>
</ux-modal>
```
